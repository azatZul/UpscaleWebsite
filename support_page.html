FAQ

What does UpScale do?

UpScale is an on-device solution to enhance and upscale your photos and videos without uploading them to a server. It ensures your privacy while delivering high-quality enhancements.

How to enhance a photo or video?

Simply select the media you want to enhance, choose the desired quality and settings, and UpScale will process it directly on your device.

Does UpScale upload files to the cloud?

No, UpScale processes all files locally on your device. Your files remain private and secure.
